# GraphRAG Factory

GraphRAG uses KnowledgeGraph evidence to ground transparent answers. KnowledgeGraph contains Entity nodes and Relation edges. Evidence reduces HallucinationRisk because the answer path can cite source documents and candidate ontology edges.

Guardrail requires Evidence before it accepts strong claims. DataGate improves source quality by removing duplicate files, short files, and noisy symbol-heavy text. HomageCore uses training traces from cleaned documents during a dry-run scaffold.
